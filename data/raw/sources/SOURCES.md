## Theoretical Framework Clarification

### McMurtry's Life-Value Onto-Axiology

The paper cites McMurtry (1999, 2013) for the Life-Value Onto-Axiology framework. These are books published by Pluto Press and are not open access.

**Open Access Alternative**: McMurtry's definitive exposition of this framework appears in:

> McMurtry, J. (2004-2011). *What is Good? What is Bad? The Value of All Values across Time, Place and Theories*. In Encyclopedia of Life Support Systems (EOLSS). UNESCO/EOLSS Publishers.

A sample chapter is archived as `mcmurtry_eolss_sample.pdf`. The full work is available through EOLSS institutional subscriptions.

**Key Concept Used in Paper**: The Primary Axiom of Value:
> "X is value if and only if, and to the extent that, x consists in or enables a more coherently inclusive range of thought/feeling/action than without it."

This axiom and the threefold capacity framework (thought, feeling, action) are documented in the archived sample chapter.
